Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

WorldCupChannel is an automated content pipeline that generates and posts FIFA World Cup 2026 match highlight videos to YouTube and TikTok. This app is operated by an individual developer.

Data Collection

This app does not collect personal data from end users. The app uses TikTok OAuth2 to authenticate a single authorized account (the channel owner) in order to post videos. No user data is stored or shared with third parties.

TikTok Integration

This app uses the TikTok Content Posting API to upload videos to the authenticated account. Access tokens are stored locally on the developer's machine and are not transmitted to any third party.
